How to Install and Use Discord on macOS

Install | Use | Video

Installation Instructions

  1. Go to the Discord website.
  2. Click the Download for Mac button.
  3. Open the downloaded Discord.dmg file.
  4. Drag the Discord app to the Applications folder.
  5. Open Discord from the Applications folder.
  6. Follow the on-screen instructions to log in or create a new account.

Usage Instructions

  • Open Discord from the Applications folder or Launchpad.
  • Log in with your account credentials.
  • To join a server, click the plus icon on the left sidebar and enter an invite link or create a new server.
  • To add friends, click on the "Friends" tab, then click "Add Friend" and enter their username and tag.
